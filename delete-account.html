Delete Your Account

How to permanently delete your Zutly account and data.

How to Delete Your Account

From the app:

  1. Open Zutly app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

⚠️ What happens when you delete your account:

  • Your profile is permanently deactivated
  • All active bookings are cancelled automatically
  • Passengers on your rides will be notified
  • Your reviews and ratings remain anonymous
  • You will be logged out from all devices
  • This action cannot be undone

Data Deleted

  • Personal information (name, phone, email)
  • Profile photo and bio
  • Government ID documents
  • Emergency contacts
  • Saved places
  • Vehicle information
  • Active session tokens

Data Retained

For legal and safety purposes, we retain:

  • Ride history (anonymized)
  • Reviews (shown as "Deleted User")
  • Transaction records (for dispute resolution, 90 days)

Can't Access the App?

If you can't access the app to delete your account, email us at:

support@zutly.in

Include your registered phone number. We'll process your request within 48 hours.
